Problem Statement

The department of the Interior was formed to protect and manage the nation's natural resources and cultural heritage. The website contains all kinds of information you would need but the amount of links and tabs are frustrating for users. It causes confusion and frustration.

Possible Solution

An updated, clean, and modern web-design. A clearer navigation menu. Using a simpler color combination that prevents tiring eyes. Using fewer tabs and links to prevent confusion and time consumption. Putting user needs at the forefront of the design.
